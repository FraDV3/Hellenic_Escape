ABOUT

I was born in the picturesque coastal region of Brittany, France, the sea became an inseparable part of my life from an early age. Childhood days were spent fishing, riding the waves while surfing and windsurfing, and setting sail on various adventures. Sailing became not just a hobby but a passionate pursuit. I honed my skills and achieved the prestigious “RYA Yachtmaster” certification.
It was during this time that I encountered one of my greatest love – Greece.
Its enchanting seas, rich culture, and warm-hearted people captured my heart. I explored the Greek waters extensively, both for the sheer joy of it and to deepen my maritime knowledge.
An unexpected opportunity arose when I joined a Greek boat rental company. This experience deepened my connection to the sea as I delved into boat maintenance and safety, all while surrounded by the beauty of the Greek islands. Today, I organize and lead sailing trips in Greece, sharing the authentic Greek experience with others. These journeys go beyond the ordinary, focusing on cultural immersion, connecting with local communities, and savoring traditional cuisine.
